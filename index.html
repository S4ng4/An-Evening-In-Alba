Tasting Menu

A culinary journey through Piemonte, perfectly paired with historic Pio Cesare wines

Aperitivo

Formaggi e Soma d'Aj

Selection of Piedmont cheeses including Toma, Castelrosso, and Gorgonzola Piccante, as well as Bresaola di Fassona & Garlic rubbed Casereeccio bread. Accompanied with Piemonte Chestnut honey & Fig jam.

Wine Pairing
Nebbiolo, Rosy (2023)
Fun, fruity expression of Langhe Nebbiolo in Rosato form

Antipasto

Bagna Cauda

Roasted organic root vegetables including beets, fennel, carrot, peppers, & parsnip served with a hot extra virgin olive oil and roast garlic fonduta dip.

Wine Pairing
Chardonnay, Pio Cesare Piodilei (2022)
Elegant oak aged Chardonnay dried fruit, vanilla, elegant & spice. A beautiful & rich expression of Chardonnay

Primi

Agnolotti del Plin

Homemade Piemonte style dumplings stuffed with veal, pork, onion, & herbs topped with butter and sage. Accompanied with a gastronomic white truffle gelato.

Wine Pairing
Barbera d'Alba, Pio Cesare (2020)
Refined oak aged Barbera produced in Alba. Features notes of ripe cherries, baking spice and delicate floral notes

Primi

Tajarini al Tartufo d'Alba

Hand rolled Piemonte style egg noodles tossed with Langhe farmer's butter & shaved Alba truffle.

Wine Pairing
Barbaresco, Pio Cesare (2019)
The essence of Barbaresco in the bottle. Notes of frutti di bosco, strawberries, violets, spice, a hint of tar and a signature minerality wrapped in perfectly balance tannins

Sorbetto di Pio Cesare Barolo agli Agrumi

To cleanse the palate by Italian Gelato Champion Stefano Biasini

Secondo

Brasato al Barolo

Braised beef slow cooked for 14 hours in Pio Cesare Barolo wine, cippolini onions, organic carrots, celery, sage, rosemary, aromatic herbs & a hint of Alba truffle salt. Served over Borlotti bean homemade polenta.

Wine Pairing
Barolo, Pio Cesare Cru: Mosconi (2018)
A masterwork French oak aged Barolo from the renowned Mosconi Cru site in Monforte d'Alba, Commune of Barolo. The Mosconi Cru provides limestone rich soil which allows Barolo to showcase great structure and intensity. The harmonious orchestration of notes including plum, cherry, rose, violet, tar & tobacco enveloped with a mineral finish. A poetic journey in Barolo.

Dolce

Piemonte Verticale di Gelato

A Piemonte focused selection of gelato by Italian Gelato Champion Stefano Biasini
"Nocciola delle Langhe" • "Gianduia" • "Fondente"

Dessert Wine Pairing
Barolo Chinato, Pio Cesare
Rich dessert wine version of Barolo infused with herbs, spices & cinchona bark
