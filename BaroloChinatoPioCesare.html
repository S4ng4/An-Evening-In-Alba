Barolo Chinato

Aromatic Wine

Producer: Pio Cesare
Base Wine: Barolo DOCG (Nebbiolo 100%)
Style: Aromatized Wine
Alcohol Content: 16.5%
Primary Botanicals: China bark (quinine), gentian root, rhubarb, cardamom seeds, cinnamon, orange peel
Serving Temperature: 16-18°C
Aging Potential: 10-15 years

Production Method

Small pieces of China bark are macerated in classic Barolo. A blend of spices is added, including gentian root, rhubarb, cardamom seeds, orange, cinnamon and other botanicals, following traditional family recipe.

Perfect Pairings

Dark chocolate desserts, aged hard cheeses (Castelmagno), panforte, digestif after meals, meditation wine with cigars

Tasting Notes

Visual

Deep amber color with ruby-red reflections, good viscosity and clarity typical of aged aromatized wines

Olfactory

Complex bouquet with intense herbal and spice aromas: quinine bitterness, gentian, cinnamon, cardamom, orange peel, with underlying Nebbiolo fruit character and medicinal herbs

Gustatory

Rich and aromatic with a distinctive bittersweet profile. Initial slight medicinal character evolves into complex fruit, herb and spice flavors with balanced sweetness and appealing bitter finish from quinine
