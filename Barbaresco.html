Tasting Notes

Visual

Beautiful ruby-tinged appearance with bright, vivid ruby red color and shimmering core. Translucent and elegant.

Olfactory

Silky and intense bouquet. Rose petal, violets, cherry, strawberry, raspberry, anise, orange peel, licorice, grilled herbs. Wild berries, blue flowers, white licorice.

Gustatory

Classic elegant and velvety style. Ripe and spicy fruit, strong but elegant tannins. Full body, great structure, good concentration. Slender, silky texture with excellent length and resonant finish.
