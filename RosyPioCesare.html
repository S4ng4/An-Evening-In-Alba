About Rosy

Named after the founder's granddaughter, this rosé comes from family-owned vineyards in Treiso, Barbaresco zone. Early harvest, gentle pressing, with brief skin maceration and 1 month aging on lees in French barriques.

Ideal Food Pairings

Excellent as aperitif, Asian spiced dishes, fresh carpaccio, seafood, light pasta dishes, and Mediterranean cuisine

Tasting Notes

Visual

Glorious amber hues with salmon pink reflections. Clear and bright with fine perlage from lees aging.

Olfactory

Spiced red berries, rose petals, touches of mountain herbs. Elegant floral notes with hints of citrus zest and mineral undertones.

Gustatory

Savory, fresh and spicy with great structure. Lively acidity balanced by full body. The finish is full of spice and red berries with elegant complexity.
